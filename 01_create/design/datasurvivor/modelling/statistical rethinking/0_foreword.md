# Foreword

This material is heavily based on the book Statistical Rethinking (2nd edition) from Richard McElreath. So much so, that I will follow its structure as much as I can, so if you are interested to read about a different angle of an idea, you can easily find the reference there.

While I try to sum up the book, I will also extend the content with materials from other places, add my own approaches and I will tailor examples more closely related to our work. 

==Sometimes you'll see highlights in this material. These are noteworthy concepts, quotes or statements from the book - or other sources - that I find worth to ponder upon.==

> Sometimes Richard uses text boxes to challenge the readers' way of thinking with an interesting example or thought process. The summary of those will be rendered in blocks like this one.
