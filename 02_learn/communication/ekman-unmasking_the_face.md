# Key Ideas
- Normally during conversation we do not look much at one another's face. (Imagine lovers. They do. Or in certain situations - eg. interrogation, an actor's face on stage, but then he "permit" us to do so.). Watching each other's faces is very intimate. Unless it's culturally apt, we don't do it. 
- The concept of _unexpected_ and _misexpected_. Surprise is greater with mixexpected events. Jokes also build on this, rather than the unexpected. (I LOVE THIS CONCEPT.)
	- Unexpected: When your friend knocks on your door at a random time. 
	- Misexpected: At a given time it's normally the food delivery that comes. But at this time it's your friend instead.
